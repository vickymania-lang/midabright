Welcome to Midabright praiz academy

Maidabright praiz academy School exists to provide the best possible education for every child irrespective of background or environment in order to produce pupils who will become key players in the larger society. Every child deserves the best and at Midabright we offer the best education at the most affordable costs because we believe that education is a right and nothing should stop a child from getting it!

We also develop our pupils/students to demonstrate resilience, respect, integrity, and independence; To be educationally prepared for the future, able to solve problems, be creative, kind, caring and compassionate, respectful to self, others, and environment, skilled in managing relationships, confidence, happiness and be active global citizens.
